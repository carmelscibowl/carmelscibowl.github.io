What is Science Bowl?

The DOE National Science Bowl is the country's premier high school science competition, sponsored by the U.S. Department of Energy. Teams of five compete in fast-paced buzzer rounds across Math, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Earth and Space Science, and Energy.
